On the southern part of the island, Rawai is considered one of the most picturesque areas in Phuket, surrounded by steep hills covered with tropical rain forests and white sandy beaches, overlooking the bay of Chalong and beautiful nearby islands.

Rawai is one of the last places still not crowded with tourists where you can enjoy a slice of peaceful traditional life. Located between Chalong Pier and Rawai Beach — just an 8 minute drive away from the world famous Nai Harn beach.
